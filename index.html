What PrepRP does

Six ways to get interview-ready

Career guidance is grounded in a dedicated dataset, with practical feedback and curated course links.

/start

Welcome experience

Greets you, sends the PrepRP poster with a clickable website button, and lists every command so you know what to try first.

PDF or text

Resume review

Upload your resume as a PDF or paste it as text and get 3 specific strengths and 3 areas to improve — each with a concrete next step.

/match

Resume-to-job matching

Add your target job and get a match percentage, the skills you already have, and the top 3 missing skills to add.

/interview

Mock interview mode

A 5–10 question mock interview, one question at a time. Get scored out of 100 across four categories, plus example stronger answers.

auto-suggested

Course recommendations

After every review or match, get real course links from a curated library including Coursera, freeCodeCamp, Google Digital Garage, and MySkillsFuture.

no command needed

General career Q&A

Ask free-text questions about internships, careers, and applications and get personalised guidance any time.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to install anything?

No. PrepRP runs inside Telegram, so you can start from the bot link on any device with Telegram.

Can I stop a mock interview early?

Yes. Type “End interview” at any time and PrepRP will wrap up the session.

Are the course recommendations real?

Yes. Recommendations come from a curated list of links from Coursera, freeCodeCamp, and MySkillsFuture.

Can PrepRP reply in another language?

Yes. Testing documented that the bot can switch to a requested language.
